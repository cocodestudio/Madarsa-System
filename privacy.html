PRIVACY POLICY

Your privacy is paramount to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Madarsa System collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our digital management platform.

Last Updated: November 28, 2025

1. Data Collection and Use

We collect necessary information to provide the core functionality of the Madarsa System:

  • Institutional Data: Madarsa name, address, and administrative contact details.
  • User Data: Names, roles (Administrator, Teacher, Student), and login credentials.
  • Academic Records: Attendance, grades, curriculum data, and performance reports.
  • Financial Data: Fee payments, expense tracking, and salary disbursements.

This data is used solely for the operation and improvement of the Madarsa System services, ensuring efficient administration and transparent academic management.

2. Log Data and Usage Information

Like most application operators, we collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our Service ("Log Data"). This Log Data may include:

  • Time and date of access and pages viewed.
  • Time spent on those pages and other statistics.
  • Browser type and version, and IP address (for security purposes).

This information is non-personal and is used for internal analysis to enhance the performance and reliability of the platform.

3. Data Security and Retention

We implement robust security measures, including data encryption (both in transit and at rest) and strict role-based access controls, to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Access is strictly limited to authorized personnel within the Madarsa institution only.

Data Retention: We retain user data for as long as the institutional account is active and for a mandatory period thereafter as required by regulatory compliance or institutional mandates.

4. Third-Party Services

We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our Service (e.g., hosting, database management, cloud services). These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

  • Hosting Providers (e.g., Google Cloud, Firebase): Used for application infrastructure and data storage.
  • Communication Services (e.g., SMS/Email APIs): Used for transactional notifications and alerts.

5. Disclosure of Personal Information

Madarsa System does not share, sell, rent, or trade any personally identifiable information with outside parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

Data may only be disclosed in the following exceptional circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: If required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  • Protecting Rights: To protect the rights, property, or safety of the Madarsa System, our users, or the public.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location and role within the institution, you may have the following rights concerning your data:

  • The right to access: Request copies of your personal data we hold.
  • The right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • The right to erasure: Request that we erase your personal data under certain conditions.
  • The right to object: Object to the processing of your personal data under certain conditions.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service primarily manages student data under the direction of the Madarsa administration. We do not directly solicit personal information from children under the age of 13. All data collection regarding minor students is managed by the Madarsa institution, which is responsible for obtaining parental consent.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Your continued use of the service after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
